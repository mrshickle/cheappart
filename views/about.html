About Us

A&E Automotive Group Inc is a leading importer and wholesaler of EFI, engine management and service components, and is a major supplier to the US automotive aftermarket. A & E Automotive Group Inc carries one of US most extensive ranges of these specialised components, from the World’s leading manufacturers, specialising in genuine and original equipment (OEM) products.

A&E Automotive Group Inc prides itself on high quality products from the world’s leading automotive parts manufacturers including Delphi, Bosch, Pierburg, Standard Motor Products, Denso, Bougicord, VDO, Walker Products, Walbro, Hitachi, Bremi, FAE, TE Automotive, Hella and Valeo, for vehicles from the USA, Europe, Japan, Korea and more

A&E Automotive Group Inc distributes throughout USA via its reputable network of specialist resellers, national distributors and leading automotive retail groups. A&E Automotive Group Inc also exports to several other countries in the region.

A&E Automotive Group Inc's offering also includes foreign auto parts. Through our ownership of Eastern Imports, we are one of the Western U.S. largest suppliers of quality parts for European and Asian automobiles. For more than 30 years, customers have relied on Eastern Imports for its vast knowledge of, and expertise in, foreign auto parts.

A&E Automotive Group Inc is also a member of the premiere Automotive Distribution Network, using the Parts Plus program. A&E Automotive Group Inc is part of the VIPAR heavy duty association, and is an ACDelco DDG member. Learn more about A&E Automotive Group Inc's groups and associations.

In an effort to help customers succeed, A&E Automotive Group Inc has put together outstanding sales, counterperson and customer service teams. A significant portion of our team members also speak Spanish.
